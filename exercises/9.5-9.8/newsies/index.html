Locals attend 30th anual ninja parade

locals of Modesto, California gathered again today for the 30th anual ninja parade.

as people lined the streets eagerly waiting, it would appear to many that nothing was occuring in the town, and a strange silence seemed to hang over as there was a wait for nothing.

however, an hour later, as people started to pack up their things and head home, seeming please with the previous events, they started to discover many things out of place, "I just found a piece of candy in my pocket." said one little boy on his way home.
